Who Are We ?

Onnit is a health and wellness company founded in 2010 by Aubrey Marcus. It started with the goal of improving human performance through a holistic approach that blends physical fitness, mental clarity, and optimal nutrition. The company is best known for its supplements, particularly those aimed at enhancing cognitive function, energy, mood, and overall well-being. Their product line includes nutritional supplements, fitness equipment, and personal care products. Some of their most popular items are Alpha BRAIN (a nootropic supplement), Total Human (a daily supplement pack), and kettlebells, among others. Onnit also emphasizes a lifestyle of functional fitness, incorporating unconventional workout tools like battle ropes and steel clubs. They promote a philosophy known as "Total Human Optimization," which includes not just supplements, but also advice on diet, exercise, and mental wellness. Aubrey Marcus, the founder, is a prominent figure in the wellness community and shares his personal experiences through podcasts and books.
